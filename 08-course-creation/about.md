# 08 · Course Creation

---

## What Is This?

Course creation is the process of packaging your knowledge into a structured, teachable product that others can learn from — and that you can sell, share, or build a business around.

It spans everything from identifying what to teach and who to teach it to, through designing curriculum, creating content, choosing a platform, pricing, and launching. A course is not just information — it's a transformation. The best ones take someone from where they are to where they want to be.

---

## Why It Matters

This is the culmination module. Everything in this repo has been building toward this. Your notes become curriculum. Your projects become case studies. Your personal brand becomes the reason people trust you enough to buy. Course creation is where knowledge becomes leverage.

## Key Tools & Platforms

- **Teachable / Thinkific** — hosted course platforms
- **Kajabi** — all-in-one course + marketing platform
- **Gumroad / Payhip** — simple digital product selling
- **Loom / OBS Studio** — screen recording and video creation
- **Notion** — curriculum planning and course content drafting
- **Canva** — course graphics, slides, workbooks

---

## Key Terms

- **Course Validation** — testing whether people will actually pay for your course before you build it
- **Curriculum** — the organised structure of lessons, modules, and content that makes up a course
- **Learning Outcome** — a specific, measurable thing a student can do after completing a lesson or module
- **Transformation** — the shift from Point A (where students start) to Point B (where they end up)
- **Ideal Student** — the specific person your course is designed for
- **Course Platform** — software that hosts and delivers your course content
- **Launch** — a planned, time-bound promotional event to drive course sales
- **Evergreen Course** — a course available for purchase at any time (not launch-dependent)
- **Cohort-Based Course** — a course where a group of students go through it together, live
- **Sales Page** — the landing page that sells your course to potential students
