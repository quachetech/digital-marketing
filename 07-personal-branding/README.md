# 07 · Personal Branding

---

## What Is This?

Personal branding is the intentional practice of shaping how you are perceived online — what you stand for, what you're known for, who you speak to, and how you show up consistently across platforms.

It's not about performing a character. It's about distilling who you actually are — your perspective, expertise, values, and voice — and making that visible and legible to the right people.

Your personal brand is the bridge between your knowledge and your audience. It's what makes people choose *you* over someone else who knows the same things.

---

## Why It Matters

Everything you've learned in this course feeds into this module. SEO helps people find you. Content marketing gives them a reason to stay. Email builds the relationship. Analytics tells you what's working. But none of it sticks without a clear, compelling personal brand anchoring it all.

As someone building courses and marketing your own work, *you are the product* as much as anything you create. This module is where the learning gets personal.

## Key Terms

- **Personal Brand** — the unique combination of skills, experience, and personality you present publicly
- **Positioning** — how you differentiate yourself from others in the same space
- **Niche** — a specific, focused area of expertise or interest you're known for
- **Brand Voice** — the consistent personality and tone you use in all your communication
- **Content Pillars** — the 3–5 core themes your content consistently covers
- **Authority** — the perception that you are knowledgeable, credible, and trustworthy in your field
- **Social Proof** — evidence that others trust, follow, or vouch for you
- **Personal Narrative** — the story of who you are, how you got here, and why it matters
- **Aesthetic** — the consistent visual feel of your brand across platforms
- **Thought Leadership** — sharing original ideas and perspectives that move conversations forward
