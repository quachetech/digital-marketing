# 04 · Email Marketing

---

## What Is This?

Email marketing is communicating directly with an audience through email — newsletters, campaigns, automated sequences, and promotional sends — with the goal of building relationships, nurturing trust, and driving action.

Your email list is the only audience you actually own. Social media followers can vanish when an algorithm changes. An email list is yours — direct, personal, and not subject to platform rules.

---

## Why It Matters

Email consistently delivers some of the highest ROI of any digital marketing channel. For course creators and personal brands, email is the backbone of launching, selling, and staying in relationship with an audience. Learning it early pays dividends across everything else you'll do.

## Key Terms

- **ESP (Email Service Provider)** — the platform you use to send and manage emails
- **Open Rate** — percentage of recipients who opened your email
- **CTR (Click-Through Rate)** — percentage who clicked a link inside the email
- **Lead Magnet** — a free resource offered in exchange for someone's email address
- **Opt-In** — when someone actively agrees to join your list
- **Email Sequence** — a series of automated emails sent in a set order
- **Welcome Sequence** — the first series of emails a new subscriber receives
- **Segmentation** — dividing your list into groups based on behaviour, interests, or demographics
- **Deliverability** — how reliably your emails actually land in inboxes (not spam)
- **Unsubscribe Rate** — percentage of people who opt out after receiving an email
