# 03 · Social Media Marketing

---

## What Is This?

Social media marketing is using social platforms — Instagram, X (Twitter), LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, Pinterest, and others — to build an audience, grow brand awareness, engage a community, and drive traffic or sales.

It's one of the most visible, fast-moving, and often misunderstood channels in digital marketing. Done strategically, it builds real relationships between brands and people. Done reactively, it's just noise.

---

## Why It Matters

Social media is where attention lives. It's where people discover new things, research before they buy, and decide whether they trust you. For personal brands and course creators especially, social media is often the primary way audiences find you, follow your journey, and eventually buy from you.

## Key Tools & Platforms

- **Meta Business Suite** — manage Facebook & Instagram
- **Buffer / Hootsuite / Later** — scheduling and analytics
- **Canva** — social graphics and templates
- **TikTok Creator Studio / YouTube Studio** — native analytics
- **Sprout Social** — advanced social management (paid)

---

## Key Terms

- **Algorithm** — the system a platform uses to decide what content to show and to whom
- **Engagement Rate** — the percentage of your audience that interacts with your content
- **Reach** — how many unique accounts saw your content
- **Impressions** — total number of times your content was displayed (includes repeats)
- **Organic Social** — content you post without paying for distribution
- **Paid Social** — boosted or advertised posts you pay to distribute
- **Social Listening** — monitoring what people are saying about your brand or topic online
- **Influencer Marketing** — partnering with creators who have an existing audience
- **UGC (User-Generated Content)** — content created by your audience about your brand
- **Content Pillars** — the recurring themes or categories your social content is built around
