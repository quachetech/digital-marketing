## Summary

Digital marketing — also known as online marketing — uses the internet and digital technologies to connect businesses with customers where they already spend most of their time. It is the digital evolution of traditional marketing, which relied on print, TV, radio, and billboards. The core channels include SEO, content marketing, email marketing, PPC advertising, social media marketing, influencer marketing, and affiliate marketing. Succeeding in the field requires a blend of creative, analytical, and technical skills.

---

## Key Concepts

- **Digital marketing = traditional marketing moved into digital spaces** — same objective, different channels and tools
- **Seven core channels** — SEO, content marketing, email marketing, PPC, social media, influencer marketing, affiliate marketing
- **Seven key skills** — communication, creative thinking, social media, data analysis, content creation, SEO/SEM, CRM and mobile marketing
- **Learning by doing** — portfolio projects are essential for practical, hands-on mastery

## Details & Deep Dives

### Traditional vs Digital Marketing

| | Traditional Marketing | Digital Marketing |
|---|---|---|
| **Media used** | Print, TV, radio, billboards | Internet, computers, mobile devices, social platforms |
| **Reach** | Geographically limited | Global |
| **Targeting** | Broad | Precise — reaches consumers where they already are |
| **Measurability** | Limited | Highly trackable and data-driven |

### The 7 Core Channels of Digital Marketing

**1. Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)**
Improving a website's ranking on search engines organically. Involves:
- Creating high-quality, relevant content
- Using targeted keywords strategically
- Ensuring fast load times and mobile-friendliness

*See module: [01 · SEO](../01_seo/README.md)*

---

**2. Content Marketing**
Creating valuable content — blogs, articles, videos — to attract and engage a target audience. Outcomes:
- Raises brand awareness
- Establishes authority and credibility in your industry

*See module: [02 · Content Marketing](../02_content-marketing/README.md)*

---

**3. Email Marketing**
Sending targeted emails to subscribers to inform, nurture, and convert. Used for:
- Announcing sales, new products, or special offers
- Building and maintaining relationships with an existing audience

*See module: [04 · Email Marketing](../04_email-marketing/README.md)*

---

**4. Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Advertising**
A paid advertising model where advertisers pay a fee each time their ad is clicked. Ads can appear on:
- Search engines (Google Ads)
- Websites (display ads)
- Social media platforms

*See module: [05 · Paid Advertising](../05_paid-advertising/README.md)*

---

**5. Social Media Marketing**
Using platforms like Facebook, Instagram, X (Twitter), and TikTok to reach and engage customers. Key outcome:
- Significantly boosts brand visibility and audience engagement

*See module: [03 · Social Media Marketing](../03_social-media-marketing/README.md)*

---

**6. Influencer Marketing**
Partnering with popular social media personalities to promote products or a brand. Why it works:
- Influencers have loyal, trust-based followings
- Their recommendations can drive meaningful traffic and sales

*Note: Covered within [03 · Social Media Marketing](../03_social-media-marketing/README.md)*

---

**7. Affiliate Marketing**
Businesses reward third-party affiliates for promoting their products. How it works:
- Affiliates share unique referral links
- They earn a commission for each sale made through their link

*See module: [10 · Affiliate Marketing](../10_affiliate-marketing/README.md)*

### 7 Key Skills for Digital Marketing

| # | Skill | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Communication & Collaboration | Marketing is about messaging — clarity and working with others is foundational |
| 2 | Creative Thinking | Standing out in a crowded digital space requires originality and fresh angles |
| 3 | Social Media Marketing | One of the most active and visible channels — practical fluency is essential |
| 4 | Data Analysis & Marketing Analytics | You cannot improve what you do not measure — data drives decisions |
| 5 | Content Creation | Content is the fuel behind almost every digital marketing channel |
| 6 | SEO & SEM | Understanding how people search and how to be found is core infrastructure |
| 7 | CRM Tools & Mobile Marketing | Managing customer relationships and reaching people on mobile devices |

## Glossary Additions

| Term | Quick Definition |
|---|---|
| Online Marketing | Another name for digital marketing — the use of internet-based channels to reach customers |
| PPC (Pay-Per-Click) | A paid advertising model where you pay only when someone clicks your ad |
| SEM (Search Engine Marketing) | Using paid ads on search engines to drive traffic — complements SEO |
| Influencer Marketing | Partnering with social media personalities who have loyal audiences to promote your brand |
| CRM (Customer Relationship Management) | Tools and strategies for managing interactions with current and potential customers |
| Referral Link | A unique trackable URL used in affiliate marketing to attribute sales to a specific affiliate |
