# 00 · Foundations of Digital Marketing

---

## What Is This?

Digital marketing is the practice of promoting products, services, ideas, or people through digital channels — the internet, social media, search engines, email, and beyond.

But before you touch any tool or platform, you need the mental models. Foundations is where you build the map before you start navigating the territory. It's the "why" and "how does this all fit together" before the "how to run a Facebook ad."

This module covers the landscape — what digital marketing is, how it differs from traditional marketing, who it's for, how it works as a system, and the core principles that run underneath every channel you'll study after this.

---

## Why It Matters

Every specialist who skips foundations ends up confused when things don't work. They know *how* to post but not *why* it's not converting. They run ads but don't understand the funnel behind them.

Foundations give you the strategic brain that makes every tactical skill more powerful. It's the difference between using tools and understanding them.

## Key Terms

- **Digital Marketing** — using digital channels to reach and influence an audience
- **Marketing Funnel** — the stages a person moves through from discovering you to buying from you
- **Target Audience** — the specific group of people your marketing is designed to reach
- **Customer Journey** — the full experience a person has with a brand, from first touch to loyal customer
- **Touchpoint** — any moment a potential customer interacts with your brand
- **Organic Traffic** — visitors who find you without you paying for the placement
- **Paid Traffic** — visitors who reach you through ads you've paid for
- **Conversion** — when someone takes the action you wanted them to take
- **KPI** — a metric you track to measure whether your marketing is working
- **TOFU / MOFU / BOFU** — Top, Middle, Bottom of Funnel — stages of audience awareness and intent
