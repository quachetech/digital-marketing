## What Is This?

Content marketing is the practice of creating and distributing valuable, relevant content to attract, engage, and build trust with a specific audience — with the long-term goal of driving profitable action.

It's not advertising. It's not shouting about your product. It's giving people something genuinely useful — an article, a video, a guide, a post — that earns their attention and trust over time. The sale, the follow, the sign-up: those are downstream results of content that actually serves people.

---

## Why It Matters

Content is the engine behind almost every other digital marketing channel. SEO needs content to rank. Social media needs content to post. Email marketing needs content to send. Personal branding *is* content. If you're building a course, you're creating content. This module is foundational to everything that follows.

## Key Terms

- **Content Marketing** — creating value through content to attract and retain an audience
- **Content Strategy** — the plan for what content to create, for whom, and why
- **Content Funnel** — using different content types for different stages of audience awareness
- **Hook** — the opening that stops someone scrolling and pulls them in
- **CTA (Call to Action)** — the specific action you want someone to take after consuming content
- **Content Repurposing** — adapting one piece of content into multiple formats
- **Evergreen Content** — content that stays relevant and useful over a long period
- **Content Calendar** — a schedule of what content to publish, when, and where
- **Thought Leadership** — content that positions you as an authority in your field
