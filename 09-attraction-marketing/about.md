# 09 · Attraction Marketing

---

## What Is This?

Attraction marketing is a philosophy and strategy built on one core idea: instead of chasing customers, you position yourself so that the right people come to you.

It flips the traditional sales model on its head. Rather than pushing your product or offer at people, you lead with value — education, inspiration, entertainment, relatability — and build enough trust and desire that people seek you out, ready to buy. It's magnetism over persuasion.

Attraction marketing draws heavily from personal branding, content marketing, and psychology. It's the methodology that underlies much of what influencers, coaches, and course creators do when they build audiences that convert.

---

## Why It Matters

For someone building a personal brand and selling knowledge products, attraction marketing is not optional — it's the game. It's the difference between constantly hustling for attention and building a presence that works while you're not actively selling. It's the long play, and it compounds.

## Key Terms

- **Attraction Marketing** — a strategy of drawing people to you through value rather than pursuing them with pitches
- **Pull Marketing** — marketing that earns attention rather than interrupting for it
- **Push Marketing** — outbound marketing that goes to people who haven't asked for it
- **Value Ladder** — a sequence of increasing value (and price) that moves someone from free content to paid products
- **Magnetic Messaging** — language so specific and resonant that the right person feels it's written for them
- **Trust Economy** — the idea that in digital marketing, trust is the currency everything is built on
- **Organic Reach** — audience growth and engagement earned without paid promotion
- **Lead Nurturing** — the process of building a relationship with a potential customer over time
