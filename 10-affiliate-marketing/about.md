# 10 · Affiliate Marketing

---

## What Is This?

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based model where you earn a commission for promoting someone else's product or service. When someone clicks your unique affiliate link and makes a purchase, you get paid a percentage of that sale — without ever creating or owning the product.

It works in two directions: you can be the **affiliate** (promoting others' products for commission) or the **merchant** (running your own affiliate programme so others promote your products for you). Both sides are worth understanding.

---

## Why It Matters

Affiliate marketing is one of the most accessible ways to generate income online, especially while building your own products. You can recommend tools, courses, and resources you actually use and get paid for it. And once you have your own courses, setting up an affiliate programme can dramatically extend your reach without additional ad spend.

## Key Tools & Platforms

- **Amazon Associates** — one of the largest affiliate networks
- **ShareASale / CJ Affiliate / Impact** — affiliate networks with many merchants
- **PartnerStack** — popular for SaaS products
- **Gumroad / Teachable** — have built-in affiliate programme features
- **Pretty Links / ThirstyAffiliates** — link management plugins
- **Rewardful** — for running your own affiliate programme

---

## Key Terms

- **Affiliate** — the person promoting a product in exchange for commission
- **Merchant / Advertiser** — the brand or creator who owns the product being promoted
- **Affiliate Link** — a unique tracked URL that attributes a sale to a specific affiliate
- **Commission** — the percentage or flat fee paid to the affiliate for a successful referral
- **Cookie Window** — the time period after a click during which a sale still earns commission
- **CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)** — a payout model where commission is earned per sale or lead
- **Revenue Share** — a commission structure based on a percentage of the sale value
- **Affiliate Network** — a platform connecting affiliates with multiple merchants
- **Conversion Rate** — the percentage of clicks that turn into sales
- **Disclosure** — the legal and ethical requirement to tell your audience when a link is affiliate-based
