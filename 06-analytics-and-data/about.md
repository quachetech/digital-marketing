# 06 · Analytics & Data

---

## What Is This?

Analytics is how you measure whether your marketing is actually working. It's the practice of collecting, reading, and interpreting data from your digital channels — website traffic, user behaviour, campaign performance, conversions — so you can make smarter decisions instead of guessing.

Data doesn't tell you what to do. It tells you what happened. Your job is to understand why, and what to do next.

---

## Why It Matters

Every other module in this course produces results that need to be measured. Without analytics, you're flying blind — spending time and money with no way to know what's working. With analytics, every campaign teaches you something. It transforms marketing from guesswork into a system you can improve deliberately.

## Key Tools & Platforms

- **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)** — website traffic and behaviour tracking
- **Google Search Console** — search performance data
- **Google Tag Manager** — managing tracking tags without code
- **Meta Insights** — Facebook & Instagram analytics
- **Looker Studio (formerly Data Studio)** — custom dashboards and reporting
- **Hotjar** — heatmaps and user session recordings

---

## Key Terms

- **KPI (Key Performance Indicator)** — a specific metric used to measure progress toward a goal
- **Session** — one visit to your website (can include multiple page views)
- **Bounce Rate** — percentage of visitors who leave after viewing only one page
- **Conversion Rate** — percentage of visitors who complete a desired action
- **Traffic Source** — where your visitors came from (search, social, direct, referral, etc.)
- **UTM Parameters** — tags added to URLs to track which campaign or source sent traffic
- **A/B Testing** — testing two versions of something to see which performs better
- **Heatmap** — a visual showing where users click, scroll, or spend time on a page
- **Attribution** — determining which marketing touchpoint gets credit for a conversion
- **Dashboard** — a visual summary of your key metrics in one place
